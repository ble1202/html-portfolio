About Me

I am a recent graduate of George Mason University with a B.S. in Information Systems. Although my UX journey started in college, I have been a creative designer my whole life. From creating scrapbooks to designing spirit wear for my high school, designing has always been one of my favorite hobbies. My career goals lie within learning how to build, contribute, and maintain design systems for organizations. By establishing a cohesive language across cross-functional teams, it serves as a resource that fosters collaboration, ensues consistency, and accelerates design and development cycles. I hope to make an impact by contributing to design systems and making everyone's work smoother and more efficient. In my free time I love spending time with friends and family, experimenting with fashion, trying new foods, and traveling (Vietnam is up next!).
